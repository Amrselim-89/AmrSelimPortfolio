BI Competency Matrix

✅ Radar Chart maps six core BI competencies — Data Modeling, DAX/SQL, Visualization, ETL/Pipelines, Governance, and Stakeholder Management — onto a symmetric hexagonal web for instant multi-dimensional comparison.

✅ Current Level (solid teal polygon) benchmarks proficiency against the Industry Average (dashed blue polygon), making capability gaps or strengths immediately visible without a separate table.

✅ Transparent fills allow both polygons to overlap and remain legible, while the contrasting stroke styles (solid vs dashed) ensure accessibility even in grayscale.

✅ Scaled 0–100% with 25-point step gridlines provides a familiar, universally understood scoring framework aligned to CMM-style maturity models.
