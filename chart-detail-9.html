Sales Pipeline Conversion Funnel

This horizontal bar chart visualizes the five stages of the sales pipeline — Leads → Qualified → Proposals → Negotiation → Closed — as a conversion funnel where volume decreases progressively from top to bottom.

✅ Funnel Shape via Horizontal Bars: By sorting bars from largest to smallest (top to bottom) and using distinct stage colours, the chart creates a natural funnel impression without requiring a specialised funnel chart library.
✅ Drop-off Visibility: The abrupt changes in bar length between stages immediately identify the highest-friction conversion points — in this case, the Proposals → Negotiation transition is the primary bottleneck.
✅ Colour-Coded Stages: Each stage uses a distinct palette colour, reinforcing stage identity and making the chart easy to reference in slide-deck annotations.
✅ Record-Count Tooltip: Hovering any bar reveals the exact record count with locale-formatted numbers, providing precise data alongside the visual impression.

Conversion funnel charts are essential in CRM reporting, digital marketing analytics, and sales operations reviews to identify where pipeline velocity is lost and prioritise improvement initiatives.
