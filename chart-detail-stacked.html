8-Quarter Revenue by Product Line

✅ Stacked Comparison: Each bar represents one quarter's total revenue, segmented by the three core product lines. Stacking allows simultaneous comparison of total revenue growth and the contribution mix of each product line over time.

✅ Consistent Growth: Total revenue has grown from $370k in Q1 2023 to $940k in Q4 2024 — a 154% increase over eight quarters — driven primarily by the BI Solutions and Consulting segments.

✅ Mix Shift: Consulting's share of total revenue has grown from 19% in Q1 2023 to 23% in Q4 2024, indicating increasing demand for advisory services alongside core BI and pipeline delivery work.

✅ Business Value: Provides finance and product leadership with a clear, longitudinal view of revenue composition trends, supporting decisions on product investment priorities and pricing strategy adjustments.
