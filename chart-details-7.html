Market Segment Distribution

✅ Polar Area Chart combines the proportional clarity of a pie chart with the magnitude encoding of a bar chart: each sector's angle represents its category, while the radial extent encodes its actual share percentage — adding an extra perceptual layer for faster comparison.

✅ Six industry verticals — Banking (32%), Retail (22%), Healthcare (15%), Gov & Public (13%), Telecom (10%), and Manufacturing (8%) — reveal the Banking sector's dominant position and the long tail of smaller segments at a glance.

✅ Animated rotation on load draws attention to the chart hierarchy, making it engaging in live presentations and stakeholder walkthroughs without being distracting.

✅ Semi-transparent fills with solid borders allow overlapping sectors to remain distinguishable, ensuring accessibility and print-quality legibility across all media.
