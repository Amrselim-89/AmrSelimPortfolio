Multi-Dimension KPI Radar Assessment

This radar chart overlays two quarterly snapshots — Q4 2024 and Q3 2024 — across six performance dimensions: Delivery, Quality, Budget, Innovation, Stakeholder Satisfaction, and Team Velocity.

✅ Six-Axis Coverage: A single radar frame communicates the full balanced scorecard at a glance, replacing six separate bar charts with one coherent visual.
✅ Quarter-over-Quarter Overlay: Overlapping filled polygons immediately reveal which dimensions improved or regressed between quarters.
✅ Dashed Baseline: Q3 uses a dashed border to distinguish it as the reference period, while Q4 (solid fill) is the primary subject of analysis.
✅ Transparent Fills: Semi-transparent area fills prevent one polygon from completely obscuring the other, preserving readability for both series.

Radar charts are the preferred format for balanced-scorecard reviews, team retrospectives, and vendor evaluations where multiple attributes must be weighted and compared simultaneously.
