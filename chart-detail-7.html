Quarterly Sales Breakdown by Region

This stacked bar chart aggregates sales performance across four regions — North, South, East, and West — for each quarter of 2024, stacking their contributions to show both individual regional performance and the total company-wide sales volume per quarter.

✅ Stacked Composition: Each bar's total height represents the company-wide quarterly revenue, while the colored segments reveal each region's proportional contribution — a dual insight impossible to convey with side-by-side bars alone.
✅ Growth Trajectory: The steadily increasing bar heights from Q1 to Q4 communicate a clear positive trend at a single glance.
✅ Color Consistency: Each region retains its assigned color across all four bars, enabling readers to track a single region's growth arc without needing a legend lookup.
✅ Index-Mode Tooltip: Hovering over any bar reveals all four regions' values for that quarter simultaneously, facilitating instant comparative analysis.

Stacked bar charts are the preferred format in regional sales reports, budget allocation reviews, and any scenario where part-to-whole relationships and cumulative totals matter equally.
