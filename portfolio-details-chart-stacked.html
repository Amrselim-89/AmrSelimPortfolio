Regional Sales — Quarterly Breakdown

📊 Overview: This stacked bar chart decomposes total quarterly sales into regional contributions (North, South, East), making it straightforward to identify both total performance and regional composition per quarter.

✅ North Region: Consistently the top contributor, growing from $320k in Q1 to $480k in Q4 — a 50% annual uplift driven by enterprise account expansion.

✅ South Region: Shows the strongest mid-year spike in Q3 ($420k), reflecting seasonal demand patterns and successful campaign execution.

✅ East Region: Steady and growing, contributing $210k to $350k — a regional market gaining traction through new channel partnerships.

✅ Key Insight: Q4 marks the highest total stack ($1.22M), confirming strong end-of-year momentum. The proportional growth of all three regions indicates balanced territorial expansion rather than concentration risk.
