Department Data Coverage Breakdown

This doughnut chart visualizes the distribution of data coverage across five organizational departments — Operations, Sales, Finance, Marketing, and HR — summing to 100% total coverage.

✅ Center Label Plugin: A custom Chart.js plugin renders the total coverage percentage and label directly inside the doughnut hole, eliminating the need for an external annotation and keeping the visual self-contained.
✅ High Cutout (68%): The wide ring keeps the centre area spacious enough for the inline label while maximising the visual arc length per segment, making small differences easier to compare.
✅ Hover Offset: Each segment pops outward on hover to isolate the selected slice without disrupting the rest of the chart.
✅ Compact Legend: Point-style legend items at the bottom use minimal vertical space, keeping the chart usable inside a constrained card layout.

Doughnut charts with center KPIs are a proven design pattern in BI dashboards where a single headline metric must be communicated alongside its constituent breakdown.
