Monthly Revenue vs Target

📊 Overview: This combo chart overlays monthly revenue bars against target bars, with a line overlay showing month-over-month (MoM) growth percentage.

✅ Revenue Bars: Actual revenue (in $k) rendered with a gradient fill — peaks visible in May and June showing strong seasonal performance.

✅ Target Bars: Set targets provide a comparative benchmark to evaluate performance gaps or achievements each month.

✅ MoM Growth Line: Plotted on a secondary Y-axis (right), this amber line highlights the percentage change from the previous month — enabling trend identification at a glance.

✅ Key Insight: April shows the highest MoM growth (+61.5%) followed by a minor dip in May, then recovery in June — typical of a healthy growth oscillation pattern.
