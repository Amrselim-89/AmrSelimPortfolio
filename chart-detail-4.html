Customer Segmentation: Engagement vs Retention

This scatter plot maps individual customers across three strategic segments — Premium, Standard, and At-Risk — using their Engagement Score (X-axis) and Retention Score (Y-axis) as coordinates.

✅ Three-Cluster Layout: Color-coded clusters make it immediately obvious which customers are high-value and well-retained vs. those at risk of churn.
✅ Actionable Quadrants: The top-right quadrant identifies loyal, engaged Premium customers; the bottom-left flags At-Risk customers needing immediate re-engagement campaigns.
✅ No Aggregation Loss: Scatter plots preserve individual data-point granularity that bar or line charts would obscure through grouping.
✅ Hover Tooltips: Each point reveals its exact engagement and retention scores on hover, enabling data-driven conversations during stakeholder presentations.

Customer segmentation scatter plots are foundational in CRM analytics, loyalty programs, and churn-prediction models to inform targeted intervention strategies.
