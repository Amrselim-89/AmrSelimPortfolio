Project Portfolio: Risk vs Value Matrix

This bubble chart plots each project in a two-dimensional risk-vs-value space, with bubble size encoding relative budget ($k). Projects are grouped into three strategic categories: Strategic, Operational, and Experimental.

✅ Three-Dimensional Encoding: X = Risk Score, Y = Business Value, R = Budget size — three independent dimensions in a single compact visual, replacing a table of three columns with an instantly interpretable picture.
✅ Quadrant Logic: High-value, low-risk bubbles (top-left) are ideal investments; high-risk, low-value bubbles (bottom-right) are prime candidates for de-prioritisation or cancellation.
✅ Category Clustering: Strategic projects cluster in the high-value region, while Experimental projects occupy the moderate-risk, moderate-value mid-zone, confirming the expected portfolio balance.
✅ Rich Tooltips: Hovering a bubble reveals precise risk, value, and budget figures, turning the visual into a quick look-up table during stakeholder reviews.

Bubble charts are the standard format for project-portfolio prioritisation, investment segmentation, and any analysis where a third quantitative variable adds meaningful context beyond a simple scatter.
