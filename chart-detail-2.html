6-Month KPI Performance Trend

This dual-series area chart compares This Year vs Last Year KPI scores over a six-month window, providing instant year-over-year context for performance reviews.

✅ Area Fill: Semi-transparent gradient fills beneath each line make it easy to see the gap between the two periods at any point in time.
✅ Dashed Baseline: The previous-year series uses a dashed stroke to visually distinguish it from the current-year solid line while keeping the legend clear.
✅ Smooth Tension: Bezier interpolation (tension 0.45) removes noise and highlights the underlying trend rather than individual data-point fluctuations.
✅ Y-Axis Banding: A fixed range of 40–100 keeps the scale consistent across reporting periods, preventing distorted perception of improvements or declines.

Year-over-year area charts like this are a staple in executive scorecards and operations reviews where trend direction matters as much as absolute values.
