Market Share by Industry Sector

This polar area chart distributes total market share across six industry verticals — Banking, Retail, Healthcare, Telecom, Energy, and Logistics — where both the angle and the radius of each segment encode the percentage value.

✅ Dual Encoding: Unlike a standard pie chart, polar area charts encode value in the radius as well as the arc angle, making small differences between segments easier to perceive at a glance.
✅ Six-Color Palette: Each sector receives a distinct hue from the design-system palette, ensuring clear differentiation without relying on pattern fills.
✅ Animated Rotation: An easeInOutQuart entrance animation draws stakeholder attention and emphasises the proportional differences as sectors grow in.
✅ Compact Bottom Legend: Point-style legend circles minimise vertical space so the chart fits comfortably within a standard dashboard card height.

Polar area charts are particularly effective when comparing a moderate number of categories (4–8) where the visual variety of the radius encoding adds analytical clarity beyond what a simple pie achieves.
