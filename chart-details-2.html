6-Month KPI Performance Trend

✅ Year-over-Year Comparison tracks the same six-month window across two fiscal years, making growth narrative immediately clear with dual filled-area curves.

✅ Teal solid area represents the current year's KPI score (percentage of targets met), while the dashed blue area shows the prior year baseline for context.

✅ Gradient fills add depth and visual weight, guiding the eye to trends rather than noise — a best-practice executive dashboard technique.

✅ Tooltips display both series simultaneously on hover so analysts can instantly quantify the improvement delta without switching views.
