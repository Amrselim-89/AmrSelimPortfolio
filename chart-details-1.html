Monthly Revenue vs Target

✅ Dual-axis Combo Chart overlaying grouped revenue bars with a Month-over-Month growth % line, giving executives both absolute values and momentum on one canvas.

✅ Revenue Bars (teal gradient) show actuals each month, while Target Bars (translucent) make over/under-performance immediately visible at a glance.
