Monthly Revenue vs Target

This combo chart overlays Revenue ($k) and Target ($k) as grouped bars, with a secondary Month-over-Month Growth % line plotted on the right axis.

✅ Revenue Bars: Show actual revenue per month with a gradient fill that visually communicates magnitude at a glance.
✅ Target Bars: Semi-transparent bars behind the revenue bars let stakeholders instantly identify over- and under-performance.
✅ MoM Growth Line: The amber overlay line tracks the percentage change month-to-month, providing momentum context without cluttering the primary axis.
✅ Dual-Axis Design: Separating revenue ($k) and growth (%) onto independent axes preserves scale integrity and eliminates misleading comparisons.

This visualization pattern is widely used in executive dashboards to communicate both absolute performance and the rate-of-change trend in a single compact view.
