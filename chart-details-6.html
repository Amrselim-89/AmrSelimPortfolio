Quarterly Sales by Region

✅ Stacked Bar Chart aggregates four regional revenue streams — Central, North, South, and West — into a single bar per quarter, making both individual contribution and total company performance scannable on one axis.

✅ Colour-coded regions (teal, blue, amber, emerald) maintain consistent visual identity across all reports in the BI suite, enabling cross-dashboard pattern recognition without needing to re-read the legend.

✅ Rounded top corners on the uppermost segment apply a polished modern styling consistent with Power BI's new visual language, improving perceived quality in boardroom presentations.

✅ Index-mode tooltips surface all four regions simultaneously on hover, allowing analysts to compare the full regional mix for any quarter in a single glance.
